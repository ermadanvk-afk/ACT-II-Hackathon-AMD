# Business Acumen & Case Studies Knowledge Base
Tags: case_study, guesstimates, rca, issue_trees, product_metrics

## 1. Guesstimates & Market Sizing
**Concept**: Testing logic and the ability to break down a massive unknown problem into smaller, logical assumptions (proxy variables).
*   **Framework**: 
    1. Clarifying questions (Geography, timeline, specific definitions).
    2. Structuring the equation (e.g., Population * % Target Demographic * % Market Penetration).
    3. Making reasonable assumptions (Round numbers for easy math).
    4. Sanity checking the final answer.
*   **AI Interviewer Instruction**: Never penalize the user if the final number is wrong. Penalize them if they jump straight to math without asking clarifying questions or if their assumptions are wildly illogical.

## 2. Root Cause Analysis (Metric Drops)
**Concept**: A core DA job is figuring out *why* a metric changed suddenly.
*   **Framework**: MECE Issue Tree (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive).
    1. Internal Factors (Bugs, tracking errors, recent app updates).
    2. External Factors (Seasonality, competitors, macro-economics).
*   **Scenario for AI to Roleplay**: "Our daily active users dropped by 15% yesterday on iOS only. Walk me through how you would investigate this."

## 3. Defining Success Metrics
**Concept**: Translating a business goal into a trackable data metric.
*   **Framework**: Primary Metric (The North Star), Secondary Metrics, Counter/Guardrail Metrics (Making sure improving one thing doesn't break another).
*   **Scenario for AI to Roleplay**: "We are launching a new 'Skip Intro' button on our video player. What metrics would you track to determine if this launch was successful?" (Good answer includes tracking if overall watch time increases, while ensuring app crashes don't increase).
