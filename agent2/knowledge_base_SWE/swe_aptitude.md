# SWE Quantitative & Technical Aptitude

## Quantitative & Numerical Ability
Assesses your ability to handle numbers quickly without a calculator.
* Basic Math: Number systems, LCM/HCF, Linear/Quadratic Equations.
* Commercial Math: Percentages, Profit & Loss, Simple & Compound Interest, Averages.
* Time & Speed: Time & Work, Pipes & Cisterns, Speed, Time & Distance.

## Logical & Analytical Reasoning
Tests critical thinking and pattern recognition.
* Deductive Logic: Syllogisms, Statement & Conclusions.
* Relational Logic: Blood Relations, Direction Sense.
* Pattern Recognition: Number Series, Alphanumeric Sequences, Puzzles.

## Verbal Ability (English)
* Reading Comprehension: Answering inferential or direct questions from passages.
* Grammar & Vocabulary: Sentence Correction, Spotting Errors, Synonyms, Antonyms.

## Technical Foundations & CS Core
* Programming & Pseudocode: Spotting syntax/logical errors, solving language-agnostic code structures.
* DSA Basics: Conceptual questions on Arrays, Linked Lists, Trees, Stacks, Queues, and Big-O time complexities.
* Core Subjects: OS, OOP, Databases (SQL), and Computer Networks.
