# SWE Core Soft Skills

Tags: `soft_skills`, `documentation`, `pr_descriptions`, `stakeholders`, `active_listening`, `agile`, `conflict_resolution`, `time_management`, `ownership`

## Technical Communication & Documentation
Writing clear documentation, well-structured commit messages, and comprehensive pull request (PR) descriptions is essential. This translates technical concepts for both peers and future maintainers.

## Stakeholder Management & Active Listening
Tailoring your communication style whether you are speaking to product managers, designers, or fellow developers. Understanding requirements thoroughly before proposing solutions or writing code by practicing active listening.

## Team Dynamics, Agile & Conflict Resolution
Modern software is built by teams. Understanding Agile/Scrum ceremonies and contributing to a positive team culture. Constructively navigating disagreements regarding architectural decisions or tech stack choices.

## Professional Effectiveness & Time Management
Balancing technical debt with new feature development. Knowing when to push for perfection vs. when to ship a Minimum Viable Product (MVP). Taking ownership by proactively identifying risks and owning your bugs.
