# ML Core Soft Skills & Business Acumen

Tags: `soft_skills`, `business_impact`, `explaining_models`, `heuristics`, `pragmatism`, `data_engineering`, `mlops`, `empathy`

## Translating Tech to Business
The ability to explain a model’s predictions, limitations, and confidence intervals to non-technical stakeholders (Product Managers, Sales, Executives) without jargon. Translating vague business requests into well-posed ML problems.

## Pragmatism & Knowing When Not to Use ML
Recognizing when a simple SQL query, heuristic, or rule-based system is a better, cheaper, and faster solution than a complex Deep Neural Network. Balancing model accuracy against inference cost and latency.

## Cross-Functional Empathy & Collaboration
Working smoothly with Data Engineers (who provide data) and DevOps/MLOps (who deploy models). Understanding their constraints is key. Resilience with messy, real-world data and handling data failures gracefully.
